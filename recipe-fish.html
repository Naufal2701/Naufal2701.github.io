Fish and chips

Fun Fact: Fish and chips became popular in England in the 1800s. It was one of the few foods not rationed during World War II. Traditionally served wrapped in newspaper (now replaced with food paper).

Ingredients

  • 2 white fish fillets
  • 2 large potatoes
  • Cup flour
  • Cup cold water
  • Salt & pepper
  • Oil for frying

How to Cook

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thick fries.
  2. Fry the potatoes in hot oil until golden and crispy, then set aside.
  3. In a bowl, mix flour, cold water, salt, and pepper to make batter.
  4. Dip fish fillets into the batter.
  5. Fry the fish in hot oil until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Serve hot fish with the crispy potatoes.
